GEOG4180/5180: Web GIS

Fall 2025

Course Objectives

The goal of this course is to introduce the basic knowledge of, and advance in, Web/Internet GIS. It covers principles, methods, and applications of Web- or Internet-based GIS. Through hands-on laboratory exercises students will develop the skill necessary to use Web GIS software packages for conducting GIS data operations (including query and mapping) and spatial analysis/modeling via Internet.
